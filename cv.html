Research Experience

Ph.D. Research Scholar

IIT Bombay, Department of Chemistry | 2021 – Present

Supervisor: Prof. Rahul Maitra

Developing variational quantum eigensolvers enhanced by generative machine learning for constructing dynamic, chemistry-inspired ansatzes. Key contributions include zero-noise extrapolated projective quantum algorithms (ZNE-PQE), ML-aided dimensionality reduction for quantum circuit design, Restricted Boltzmann Machine (RBM)-assisted ansatz construction, and shallow-depth adaptive ansatz construction frameworks targeting NISQ hardware. Implementation primarily in Qiskit with classical ML components in Python (PyTorch / scikit-learn). Experiments on IBM Heron R2 and R3 quantum processors (up to 52 qubits).
